The LSPG ROM formulation

LSPG projection corresponds to minimizing the (weighted) -norm of the time-discrete residual over the trial manifold. Hence, the starting point for this approach is the residual ODE formulation discretized in time with an arbitrary time-discretization method.

LSPG projection is derived by substituting the approximate state in the time-discrete residual and minimizing its (weighted) -norm, which yields a sequence of residual-minimization problems

with initial condition . The matrix with denotes a weighting matrix that can enable hyper-reduction.
